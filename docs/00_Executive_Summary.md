## 1. Where we are today

Our plot platform has cracked a formula the Indian market largely lacks: a **consumer-facing, builder-branded app** with an interactive project map, live plot availability, transparent pricing, a broker (associate) portal, booking tracking, resale, and bilingual EN/हिंदी UI. Almost every competitor (Sell.Do, DaeBuild, HomeLead, LeadSquared) sells an **internal CRM for the builder's staff**. We sell the builder a **branded digital experience for their buyers** — and the back office comes with it. That is our wedge, and it transfers directly to vertical real estate.

## 2. The opportunity

Apartments, commercial complexes and malls are far larger and stickier markets than plots:

- **Higher ticket sizes** (₹40L–₹5Cr+ per unit) → builders will pay more for software that moves sales.
- **Longer lifecycle** (2–5 years booking-to-possession) → 24–60 months of engagement per buyer vs weeks for a plot. More lifecycle = more modules = more revenue per client.
- **Heavier compliance** (RERA construction-linked payment plans, quarterly progress uploads, escrow audits) → compliance automation is a purchase trigger, not a nice-to-have.
- **Malls/commercial** are almost untouched by modern Indian software — dominated by expensive foreign ERPs (Yardi, MRI) or legacy Indian ERPs (In4Suite, Property-xRM). Nothing consumer-grade, nothing white-label, nothing mobile-first exists for mid-market mall owners.

## 3. Our strategic thesis

> **"The builder's own super-app for the entire building lifecycle: Discover → Book → Pay → Watch it rise → Take keys → Live/Lease."**

Three principles carried over from plots, now applied vertically:

1. **Radical transparency sells.** Live unit availability, an itemised cost sheet with zero hidden charges, and photo-verified construction progress turn the #1 Indian buyer anxiety ("will I get cheated?") into the builder's #1 marketing weapon.
2. **The buyer app IS the CRM.** Every buyer action (viewing units, downloading cost sheets, EOI payments) feeds the builder's pipeline automatically. No data entry.
3. **WhatsApp + Hindi + simplicity.** Indian real estate runs on WhatsApp and family decisions. Every notification, document and payment link must work on WhatsApp; every screen must work for a 55-year-old parent co-deciding the purchase.

## 4. What we will build (headline features)

**Residential vertical (apartments):**
- **Interactive 3D Tower Explorer** — the vertical equivalent of our plot map: tap tower → floor → unit; live availability colours; view-from-balcony; sun-path simulation; Vastu filters.
- **Digital booking engine** — EOI/token payment online, 15-minute unit hold, instant allotment letter, e-sign + e-stamp agreement.
- **Live Cost Sheet Generator** — base price + floor-rise + PLC + GST + stamp duty + registration + maintenance corpus, all itemised, downloadable, shareable on WhatsApp.
- **Construction-Linked Payment Autopilot** — RERA CLP schedule per unit, milestone-triggered demand letters, buyer ledger ("Digital Khata"), online payment, bank/loan disbursement tracking.
- **"Watch Your Home Rise"** — geo-tagged weekly photos, drone timelapse, milestone verification that gates demand letters (buyers trust demands because they can *see* the slab is done).
- **Digital Possession & Snagging** — inspection checklist, photo snag list, rectification tracking, OC status, digital key handover.
- **Referral engine** — activate the builder's existing families (Riyasat has 11,000+) as a sales channel.

**Commercial & mall vertical:**
- **Interactive Stacking Plan / Mall Map** — floor-wise leasing map with live availability, tenant mix zoning, frontage/footfall attributes per unit.
- **Lease lifecycle manager** — term sheet → LOI → lease with escalations, revenue-share/percentage rent, lock-ins, renewals.
- **CAM & billing engine** — pro-rata CAM, electricity/HVAC sub-metering, bulk invoicing, GST-compliant.
- **Tenant portal** — invoices, payments, tickets, sales reporting (for revenue-share), marketing calendar.
- **Investor module** — for strata-sold commercial (units sold to investors with assured rental/lease management), a huge Jaipur/Tier-2 pattern nobody serves well.

## 5. What's genuinely unique (see Doc 07)

The market has CRMs and the market has 3D-render vendors. **Nobody in India combines**: white-label buyer app + live 3D inventory + payments/ledger + verified construction progress + possession + post-sales, in one product a mid-market builder can afford. Our sharpest unique features: photo-verified milestone→demand-letter gating, the buyer "Digital Khata" ledger, sun-path & Vastu explorer, price-lock EOI, NRI mode, and the strata-investor rental module for commercial.
